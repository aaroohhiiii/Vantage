## ECONOMICS.md

### What a converted lead is worth to Credex

Assumptions :
- Credex sells AI credits at a discount (e.g., 20–40% off retail)
- Average credit purchase: $5,000–$15,000 per deal
- Gross margin on credits: ~15–25% (buying distressed credits at deeper discount)
- Assume average deal: $8,000, margin: 20% → $1,600 gross profit per customer
- Customer repeat purchase rate: assume 60% buy again within 6 months
- LTV estimate: $8,000 * 1.6 purchases = $12,800 revenue, $2,560 gross profit

### CAC at each channel

Channel: Hacker News post → estimated 500 visits, 150 audits, 40 leads, 4 consultations, 1 customer. CAC: $0 cash, ~5 hours of writing. Effective CAC at $100/hr opportunity cost: $500.

Channel: Twitter DMs → 30 DMs, 5 responses, 2 audits, 1 lead, ~0.2 customers.
CAC: $0 cash, ~2 hours. Effective CAC: $200.

Channel: Newsletter mention → 2,000 readers, 100 clicks, 30 audits, 8 leads, 2 consultations, 0.5 customers. CAC: ~$200 (if paid), $0 if organic.

### Conversion funnel

Audit completed → email captured: target 30%
Email captured → consultation booked: target 10–15%
Consultation booked → credit purchase: target 25–33%

Overall: 1 in ~100 audits becomes a customer.

### What drives $1M ARR in 18 months

$1M ARR = ~$83k/month revenue
At $8k average deal: ~10 new customers/month
At 1% audit-to-customer rate: need 1,000 audits/month
At 30% audit-to-email rate: need ~3,300 page views/month driving audits

This is achievable through:
- Referral viral loop (shareable URLs)
- Monthly HN posts as new pricing data updates
- Credex existing customer distribution
- SEO from PRICING_DATA content (people googling tool prices)

Payback period: if CAC is $500 and gross profit per customer is $1,600 → payback in ~3.75 months. Very healthy for a B2B SaaS tool.
